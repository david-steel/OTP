What We Do

Full-spectrum IP protection

From first invention disclosure to portfolio monetization and M&A due diligence.

Patent Prosecution

Draft and prosecute patent applications that withstand scrutiny and maximize claim scope. We write patents that protect real competitive advantage.

Patent Mining & Strategy

Systematic invention harvesting sessions with your engineering team. We find the patentable innovations hiding in your codebase and roadmap.

In-House Counseling

Embedded IP counsel without the full-time salary. Strategic guidance, FTO analysis, and ongoing portfolio management at a fraction of the cost.

Global Patent Protection

PCT filings, national phase entries, and foreign prosecution managed through our network of trusted international associates in 40+ countries.

IP Monetization

Turn your patent portfolio into revenue through licensing, assertion, and strategic transactions. We identify and execute monetization opportunities.

IP Due Diligence

Comprehensive IP audits for M&A, fundraising, and strategic planning. Know exactly what you own, what it's worth, and where the gaps are.
