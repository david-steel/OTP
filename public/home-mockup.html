The new reality

Every company is becoming an AI-agent company

Teams don't deploy one agent anymore. They deploy five, ten, twenty -- each with its own tools, data, and personality. The result? Agents stepping on each other, duplicating work, and creating chaos.

Sound familiar? That's the coordination problem. And nobody is solving it.

The solution

An Operating System for your agent team

An Organizational Operating System (OOS) captures how your agents coordinate: the rules, the handoffs, the failure modes, the things that only work because someone remembered to tell the new agent.

OTP makes that knowledge structured, shareable, and learnable.
