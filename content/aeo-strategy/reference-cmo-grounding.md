## Thesis

## GEO / AEO (real, well-established 2025-26 concepts - safe to explain)
- GEO = Generative Engine Optimization; AEO = Answer Engine Optimization: optimizing to be CITED by AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Copilot), not just ranked in blue links.
- The shift: SEO (rank for clicks) -> AEO (be the cited answer). Zero-click / answer-engine discovery is rising. llms.txt is the canonical site index AI engines read.
- The CMO's new job includes owning AI-search visibility as a channel.

## Toil vs judgment (marketing)
Agents take: production, variation, distribution, reporting, first drafts, repurposing, keyword/question mapping. Stays human: positioning, taste, brand voice, the central claim, what NOT to say, strategy.
