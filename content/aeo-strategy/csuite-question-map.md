This map targets the executive buyer: the CFO, CEO, CIO, COO, CHRO, CMO who is being asked, by their board and by the market, "what does my job become when agents do the operational work."

**Strategy:** own the AI-engine citation for "what does the [role] do in the age of AI" for every C-suite seat, before McKinsey, Deloitte, Gartner, HBR, and the business schools own it. The schools are now teaching this. The advisory firms are publishing on it. None of them are running a live hybrid human-and-agent chart and reporting from inside it. We are. That is the wedge.

**Mission line that every post defends:** let agents carry the operational work, so people are free for the work that matters. For the CFO this is literal and measurable. Finance is the function with the clearest line between toil (reconciliations, close, reporting, data prep) and judgment (capital allocation, risk, strategy). The CFO is the proof case for the whole thesis.

**Sequence:** CFO first and deepest (proof case). Then CEO, CIO, COO, CHRO, CMO reuse the identical engine. Target for the first ship batch: 50 posts, CFO-weighted.

**Cadence + voice:** same engine as `/OTP-AEO`. David Steel's voice. Declarative. No em dashes. Short paragraphs. Concrete examples from the live Sneeze It chart (Janine, Dash, Tally, Dirk, Radar, Pulse, Bogdan, etc.). One contrarian or clarifying central claim per post. Ends with the OTP MCP install snippet. Every post runs the voice gate before it ships.
